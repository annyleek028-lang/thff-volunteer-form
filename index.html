Eligibility & Commitments

  • 18+ as of Oct 28, 2026.
  • Commit to 4 shifts minimum (1/day, ~4h each) during Oct 29 – Nov 8, 2026.
  • Good English comprehension (other languages are an asset).
  • Most roles require standing/walking for the whole shift.
  • Mandatory orientation & training in Sept/Oct 2026 (online + in‑person).
  • Inclusive environment — applicants from all backgrounds are welcome.

Volunteer Roles (summary)

Spooky Cosplayer
Interact safely (non‑physical), stay in character, comfortable with masks/makeup; performance background is an asset.
Cinema Attendant
Greet, scan tickets, usher, flow control, counts; placement varies by venue needs.
Accessibility Support
Assist with devices (e.g., D.A.S.), guide to quiet spaces, sensory kits; ASL knowledge preferred; headset/radio; First Aid is an asset.

Full descriptions provided in training materials.
